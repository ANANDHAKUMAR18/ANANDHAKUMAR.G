--Making of noodles--

Let's see how to make noodles in 2 mins:

  • First,take a bottle of water
  • Next,cut the noodles packet
  • Pour the water and put the noodles in the cup in which you are gonna cook
  • Keep the flame a bit high
  • Off the stove and serve the noodles in the plate and start enjoying the food
